J. Jonah Jameson
Jameson is a charismatic but demanding leader who is always looking for the next big exclusive. He is famous for his passionate dislike of Spider-Man, whom he considers a criminal and a threat to the city. Although he can be tough on his team and demand results, J. Jonah Jameson also has a human side and shows concern for the well-being of his employees. Despite his quirks, his dedication to journalism and his ability to drive the team toward excellence are admired by many at the Daily Bugle.
Joseph "Robbie" Robertson
Robbie is a key part of the Daily Bugle newsroom. He is an editor respected and admired by everyone in the office because of his experience, wisdom and leadership. He always makes sure the stories are accurate, well-researched, and presented fairly. His dedication to journalistic rigor is an inspiration to everyone at the Bugle.
Benjamin "Ben" Ulrich
Ben Urich is a veteran journalist at the Daily Bugle, known for his ability to investigate and tell hard-hitting stories. He is a dedicated professional who always seeks the truth and is willing to go beyond to uncover the reality behind the headlines. Ben is respected for his integrity and his ability to stand firm in his convictions, even when he faces external pressures.
Peter B. Parker
Peter parker is an exceptional boy with an exceptional eye, every time we need a good photo (mainly of spiderman) he is there to take it. But Parker's talents do not end there, his help with computers and technology is always appreciated, although sometimes he is somewhat nosy and defends the web crawler too much, he is a great person
Elizabeth "Betty" Brant
Betty is known for her professionalism and her ability to remain calm even in the most stressful situations. She has an impeccable work ethic and always ensures that her work is well researched and accurately presented. In addition to being an excellent journalist, Betty is a very kind and compassionate person. She is always willing to offer support to her coworkers and she is highly respected by everyone in the newsroom.
Edward "Ned" Leeds
Leeds is always on top of the latest news and he's known for his ability to thoroughly investigate any story that falls into his hands. Ned is a friendly person and is always willing to help his co-workers. He's somehow always cracking jokes and maintaining a positive atmosphere even in the most stressful of times.
